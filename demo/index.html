      About

      Unified game library for Android

      GameFibre – Rediscover your games

      We hoard games, stare at our libraries and can’t decide what to play. GameFibre brings your Steam, GOG, and Epic libraries together in a beautifully designed space built for exploration and discovery – making choosing your next game fun again 😊.

      A joyful way to explore your backlog

      Turn your library into a playground. Shuffle lists randomly, quiz yourself on release dates and critical reception, or solve a slide puzzle of customized box art. Discovering your next game is full of surprises.

      Made with ❤️

      Everything you need. Nothing you don’t.

      One library

      Bring Steam, GOG, and Epic together into one unified and visually rich space.

      Customize

      Apply artwork from SteamGridDB, set favorites and create your own lists. Make your library truly yours.

      Discover

      Browse by review scores, HowLongToBeat playtime estimates or installation size. Shuffle your library for surprise picks.

      Friends Games

      Discover games from friends and save the ones you love to your wishlist.

      Offline

      Your collection, lists and box art visuals stay offline, always accessible even without a network or if services expire.

      Instant Play

      Launch games directly from within GameFibre, using external clients like GameNative or GameHub Lite.

      Privacy first

      No tracking. No profiling. No ads. No catch. Your data is kept on your device only, available to export at your convenience.
